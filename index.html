RESEARCH PAPER

A Conceptual Framework for an AI-Powered Triage System in Indian Lower Courts

This research paper presents a comprehensive framework for implementing an AI-powered triage system designed to address the overwhelming case backlogs in Indian lower courts. The system leverages machine learning and natural language processing to automate case categorization, prioritization, and routing, potentially revolutionizing judicial efficiency in India.
